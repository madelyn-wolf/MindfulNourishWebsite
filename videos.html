Videos & Resources

Websites

Helpful, science-based nutrition and fitness sites offering guidance on healthy eating, portion control, and balanced habits.

  • MyPlate.gov

    A U.S. Department of Agriculture resource that helps you build balanced meals, understand food groups, and make healthy eating part of your daily routine.

  • EatRight.org

    Created by the Academy of Nutrition and Dietetics, this site offers science-backed nutrition advice and practical tips for healthy living.

  • Nutrition.gov

    A federal government website with reliable dietary guidance, tools, and resources to support lifelong healthy habits.

  • Muscle & Strength

    A comprehensive library of structured workout programs, training guides, and fitness tips designed to help you build muscle, burn fat, and improve strength, from beginner to advanced levels.

Dollar Tree Dinners

While Rebecca started her channel focused on making nutritious meals with Dollar Tree ingredients, she now also makes realistic and wholesome meals and menu planning ideas. She shares budget-friendly tips and her recipes are outstanding!

Ian Fujimoto

Ian is a chef that shares wonderful recipes! He has also been on a health journey and his humor combined with his chef skills make him a fantastic resource for people wanting to start making nutritious and tasty meals in the kitchen!

Grow with Jo

Grow with Jo is a long-time creator that focuses on workouts that can be done at home. Her content and guidance has helped many people embark on their fitness journies. Her videos make fitness fun and are perfect for beginners, or people that want to get a workout from the comfort of their own home!
